How to Train Your Dragon in 10 Days

By Champi Brahma

I like dragons… or at least, the ones that live in my house disguised as cats. They may not breathe fire, but they do shoot me fierce stares when I’m late with dinner. They don’t guard piles of gold, but they’ll defend the sunny spot on the couch like it’s a sacred treasure.

STEPS:

Day 1 — The Summoning Ritual

Start by creating a positive association: the sound of treats, a gentle call, and calm energy. Repeat the cue and reward generously. Patience wins over forced hugs.

Day 2 — Learning Dragon Speech

Observe tail flicks, ear rotations, and slow blinks. Mirror calm blinks and learn what each signal means.

Day 3 — The Sacred Offering

Offer high-value treats by hand or on a special plate. If your dragon walks away — try again later.

Day 4 — Play Combat

Use toys to simulate prey. Keep sessions short and rewarding.

Day 5 — The Great Perch

Provide high vantage points to make your dragon feel secure.

Day 6 — The Taming Touch

Offer gentle chin scritches if welcomed. Keep it short and calm.

Day 7 — The Command Trial

Teach simple cues like Come, Sit, or Paw with treats.

Day 8 — Mini Adventures

Introduce safe new experiences like harness walks or puzzle feeders.

Day 9 — The Dragon Dance

Keep a consistent daily routine for feeding, play, and rest.

Day 10 — The Rider & The Dragon

Celebrate your bond — your dragon may be independent, but trust is now stronger.

“Take care of your dragons.”

Bonus: The Dragon Care Checklist

  • High-quality food & fresh water
  • Regular play (at least twice daily)
  • Safe vertical spaces
  • Positive reinforcement
  • Routine vet checkups
